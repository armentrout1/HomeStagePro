# RoomStager Pro SEO Content Roadmap

## Target Audiences
1. **Real Estate Agents** – Residential listing agents who need fast, affordable staging to refresh MLS listings and win more seller appointments.
2. **Property Investors** – Fix-and-flip or build-to-rent operators focused on compressing holding time by modernizing property visuals before resale or leasing.
3. **Short-Term Rental (STR) Hosts & Managers** – Airbnb/VRBO operators optimizing occupancy and ADR with on-brand, lifestyle-driven staged interiors.
4. **Real Estate Photographers & Media Pros** – Service providers seeking higher-ticket add-ons (virtual staging, decluttering, day-to-dusk) for their client base.

## Topic Clusters
1. **Virtual Staging Mastery** – Tutorials, workflows, AI prompts, before/after case studies, and comparisons to traditional staging.
2. **Cost, ROI & Pricing Strategy** – Budget breakdowns, package calculators, ROI case studies per audience segment, and pricing psychology.
3. **Real Estate Photo Excellence** – Guides to capture-ready homes, photo editing, listing media best practices, and gallery inspiration.

## Repeatable Content Brief Template
- **Primary Keyword:** `{{keyword}}`
- **Search Intent:** (Informational / Commercial / Transactional)
- **H1:** `{{proposed headline}}`
- **Suggested URL Slug:** `/{{keyword-theme}}/`
- **Meta Title Draft:** `{{60c max, include brand tag}}`
- **Meta Description Draft:** `{{155c summary + CTA}}`
- **Page Outline:**
  - **H2 #1:** Problem/need framing tied to the audience.
    - **H3:** Supporting evidence or data.
  - **H2 #2:** RoomStager Pro solution (features, workflow, differentiators).
    - **H3:** Step-by-step, package table, or timeline.
  - **H2 #3:** Proof (case studies, testimonials, gallery embeds).
    - **H3:** Audience-specific success metrics.
  - **H2 #4:** Implementation + CTA.
    - **H3:** Next steps, contact, or pricing summary.
  - **H2 #5:** FAQ block (structured data-ready).
- **Internal Links:**
  - **From:** Existing blogs/resources that should point to this page.
  - **To:** Relevant money pages, gallery, pricing, or signup forms.
- **CTA Placement:** Note hero CTA, mid-page banner, and end-of-page form or button.
- **Required Images:** Before/after pairs, device mockups, audience-specific lifestyle imagery.
- **FAQ Section:** 3-5 schema-ready Q&As addressing objections, compliance, and workflow.
